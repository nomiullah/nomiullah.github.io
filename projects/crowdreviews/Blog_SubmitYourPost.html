More Information About Our Contributor Program

Thank you for your interest in becoming a contributor at CrowdReviews.com. We are incredibily selective and open to having thought leaders contribute insightful content on our website.

Please review our contributor requirements and connect with us if you are interested in contributing.

Contributor Requirements:

  • 2 articles per month.
  • Minimum 12 month commitment.
  • Content must be between 400 - 1,000 words. If over 1,000 please connect with a representative.
  • Title for articles no longer than 15 words.
  • Articles should be submitted in text format (Microsoft Word, OpenOffice, etc. Not PDF)
  • Articles can include up to 3 images in the article, and 1 image for the article publication (Images need to be in .jpeg .gif, or .png format and at least 300DPI).
  • Article Submissions must include: Author’s Name, Company name, Address, Email, and Brief Bio of Author (No more than 100 words).
  • You are able to add a link back to your site in the Author Bio.
