Jameson Frere Employee

POSITIVE Review
Extremely Professional!
Reviewed on March 19, 2015

Hired For: Search Engine Optimization in 2011

What was the best part about dealing with SEO Discovery?

There were positive client testimonials of their search engine optimization services available on their home page but in my opinion it would be more significant if they named these people along with their companies. There were positive client testimonials available on their portfolio page along with several case studies in which I was able to verify page one rankings on Google for a number of their clients which was great to see.

What was the worst part about dealing with SEO Discovery?

Their SEO Woorank was forty three out of one hundred which was among the lowest of any company while doing my due diligence. While not a deal breaker as it doesn't reflect client SEO success directly, I appreciated seeing companies which led by example through their own search engine optimization services. They were not listed or accredited by the Better Business Bureau which would help facilitate significant trust in prospects. They were not Google Partner certified when I checked which would be a useful way to help validate their expertise in my opinion. Their website was professional overall yet there were minor text formatting and spelling issues, redundant links (which clicked but did not enable any movement) and a missing graphic on their website which were minor from a visitor standpoint, yet I understand search engine algorithms take this into consideration when factoring their rankings. Therefore, I was curious as to how their attention to detail would affect client campaigns in this regard.

Overall Feedback

This company provided pricing for their SEO packages on their home page and were extremely competitive. Certainly among the lowest I had seen out of all of the companies during my research. And while they had not met all of my presales criteria and there were minor issues in regards to attention to detail on their website, the fact I was able to verify some of their page one rankings along with the positive testimonials which were attached to these case studies was enough credibility that I looked forward to learning more about their service. And though they were among the finalists for our business, with a bit more attention to detail of their own SEO example perhaps we would have chosen this company, however, we went in another direction. Although I look forward to circling around in the future and seeing if they are the best fit for our other projects.
