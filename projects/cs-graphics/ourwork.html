ChildProtect Canada provides a range of products and services that enable families to better cope when a child is diagnosed with and survives a serious illness or medical condition.

We designed their new logo to appear strong, solid and reliable while reflecting the company’s focus on children. We chose primary colours, which are most often associated with children, and reinforced the aspect of “protection” by combining an image of a healthy, happy child with a shield symbol. The word “Protect” was expressed in bold type to create a strong relationship between it and the shield. As a result, the logo’s meaning and company name resonate with the viewer instantly.
