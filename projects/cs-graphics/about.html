Creative Director & President

For over 20 years Christine has been responding to the needs of clients with sophisticated and appropriate design solutions.

Christine draws out and determines exactly what the client’s ultimate objective is and aims to not just meet it, but exceed it! This client-centric approach has differentiated CS-Graphic Design in the industry and kept clients coming back from the time CS opened its doors!
