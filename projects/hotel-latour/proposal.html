The Proposal

Hotel La Tour Ltd are proposing to build a new, luxury hotel in
Central Milton Keynes, that will include:

250 Bedrooms

Our range of luxury hotel rooms will be designed to suit everyone, reflecting our focus as hoteliers.

Rooftop Restaurant

The focal point will be our rooftop restaurant, providing unprecedented views over Central Milton Keynes and Campbell Park, publicly accessible via an external glass elevator.

Window to Milton Keynes

Be it for a wedding party or simply to relax after business or leisure, guests will be able to enjoy views over Central Milton Keynes and Campbell Park.

Conference Facilities

Our conference facilities will be capable of accommodating a wide range of events, from small business meetings to product launches and banquets.

Gym, Spa and Swimming Pool Facilities

Guests will be able to exercise or simply relax in the spa.
