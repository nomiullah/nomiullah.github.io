The Proposal

Our proposals will redevelop the coach park to the east of John Lewis car park in Central Milton Keynes, creating a new luxury hotel under the Hotel La Tour brand.

Our emphasis is on quality, from building design to facility provision and service experience, we are committed to providing first-class customer service.
