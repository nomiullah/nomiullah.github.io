About Hotel La Tour

We are a British, privately owned Hotel Company with one previous hotel in
Birmingham and are developing proposals for a new hotel within Milton Keynes.

We operate at the upper end of the hotel market, with our hotel in Birmingham achieving a 4 star plus standard.

We are exceptionally proud of our previous Birmingham hotel. Our focus on creating lasting memories and exceptional experiences for guests is widely recognised – our rating on Trip Advisor is second to none for a full service hotel in the city.

In Birmingham, our hotel had a modern design, with leading conference facilities and a fantastic food offer, having partnered with Marco Pierre White to offer food that was not only exciting to our hotel guests, but to the general public as well.

More details and images of our previous hotel in Birmingham are available here.

It is our focus on customer service and our meticulous attention to detail that we will bring to our new hotel in Milton Keynes. The design will be stylish and will create unprecedented views of Central Milton Keynes and Campbell Park that all of our guests will be able to enjoy.

It is for this reason that we are so excited to open a new hotel in Milton Keynes. We feel that the range of business and leisure opportunities in Milton Keynes, combined with a current shortage of luxury hotels and the continued development of the city centre, offer the perfect opportunity for Hotel La Tour.

We are hoteliers, not developers, and are making a long-term investment in the future of Milton Keynes – we hope that you share our excitement at this opportunity.
