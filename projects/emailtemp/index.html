How ella forums work

Expert Speakers

Each monthly meeting will include a workshop from an expert speaker to talk on issues pertinent to the Third Sector and to members individual learning needs.

One-to-one Coaching/Mentoring

You will receive valuable time with the Chair or someone chosen by the Chair, with the right skills and knowledge to work with you to address issues and support progress. Always face-to-face at a place chosen by you.

Peer Group Learning

At monthly meetings members will have the opportunity to share challenges, issues or opportunities and gain support and advice from other leaders.
