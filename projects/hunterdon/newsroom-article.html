Hunterdon Medical Center Among America’s Best Hospitals to Have a Baby

Joanne Bulmer, R.N., a registered nurse takes the vital signs of newborn, Aislinn Grace of Lambertville, at the Maternity and Newborn Care Center at Hunterdon Medical Center.

Published January 26, 2015 

Women Vote Hunterdon Obstetrics

Hunterdon Medical Center has received the 2015 Women’s Choice Award® as an America’s Best Hospitals for Obstetrics . This evidence-based designation is the only award that identifies the country’s best healthcare institutions based on robust criteria that consider female patient satisfaction, clinical excellence, and what women say they want from a hospital.

The list of over 350 award winners represents hospitals, including Hunterdon Medical Center that women can feel confident in choosing for their maternity needs.

Expert Care

“The expert level of care and attention that is provided to new mothers by our board certified physicians at Hunterdon Medical Center is one of the many reasons that we have been awarded the Women’s Choice Award. From prenatal care and education, to labor and delivery, and even after you bring your baby home, our Magnet–recognized nurses and experienced board-certified physicians are here to help new mothers every step of the way,” stated Ardath Youngblood, MSN, Director of the Maternity and Newborn Care Center.

“New moms have many choices when it comes to obstetrics. Now they can make informed decisions based on the recommendations of other moms who have experienced that hospital first hand,” says Delia Passi, CEO and Founder of the Women’s Choice Award, and former publisher of Working Woman and Working Mother magazines. “Most importantly, when a woman sees the Women’s Choice Award at her local hospital, she’ll know the hospital values her experience as a critical component of care for her and her loved ones,” added Passi.

New Renovations

Hunterdon Medical Center recently renovated the Maternity and Newborn Care Center to ensure the utmost comfort and convenience for you and your family. The twenty private and spacious rooms have an upscale hotel feel with a spa-like quality that will make the birthing and recovery process comfortable.

Each room comes complete with:
  • Memory Foam mattress for mom
  • A sleep sofa for the partner
  • Flat-screen television
  • Private bathrooms

Separate rooms for labor and delivery

The America’s Best Hospitals for Obstetrics scoring process is unique in that it is the only national list that focuses on female patient satisfaction. Only the hospitals who qualify for this highly selective designation provide the highest level of obstetric services based on their exceptional scores for patient recommendation, as provided by data reported by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services in Hospital Consumer Assessment of Healthcare Providers and Systems (HCAHPS) surveys, as well as an analysis that weighs criteria identified as the most important to women for patient satisfaction, including early elective deliveries.

For more information on the Maternity and Newborn Care Center at Hunterdon Medical Center, visit www.hunterdonhealthcare.org/maternity or call 908-788-6667.

About the Women’s Choice Award®

The Women’s Choice Award sets the standard for helping women to make smarter choices for themselves and their families. The company and its awards identify the brands, products and services that are most recommended and trusted by women. The Women’s Choice Award is the only evidence-based quality designation that drives consumer and patient appreciation through education, empowerment and validation. Additionally, they recognize those that deliver a recommendation-worthy customer experience. Visit www.WomensChoiceAward.com to learn more.
