Heart and Vascular Services of Hunterdon Medical Center

combines a knowledgeable staff of physicians and specialists with state-of-the-art technology to bring you the best cardiovascular care in Hunterdon and its surrounding counties. Our superior staff and technological support enable us to diagnose heart and vascular disease and perform interventional procedures on patients suspected of heart or vascular disease. Our heart and vascular services team has the experience and expertise to give you the best possible care and guide you through recovery and beyond.
