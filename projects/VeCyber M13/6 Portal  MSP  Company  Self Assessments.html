CSC #01 - Inventory of Authorized and Unauthorized Devices

Severity Level: Critical

Does your organization determine if new devices have been connected since the last inventory?

You answered: Inventory available, but greater than 3 month old.

Why is this important?

Unsecured devices could allow mailcious actors to bypass security. Establishing a hardware inventory program helps reduce this risk.

Best practice advice

  1. Create an inventory of authorized devices as a baseline
  2. Periodically compare (at least monthly) baseline to current inventory to discover new devices
  3. Confirm new devices are authorized and have been securely configured.
  4. Authomate this task and set up an alert if new devices are encountered to ensure rapid detection and lower organization risk.
  5. New device discovery alerts organization to a potential security risk.
