About Nautilux Canvas

Welcome to our website! Nautilux is a family owned and operated company that prides itself in fabricating high quality custom marine canvas and providing unsurpassed customer service. It is the small things such as returning phone calls and showing up for appointments (on time) that we take very seriously.

Nautilux provides a range of services from simple dash or helm covers, to high-end luxury yacht enclosures. We also offer marine upholstery, such as coaming pads, cockpit cushions, and reupholster of existing seating.
