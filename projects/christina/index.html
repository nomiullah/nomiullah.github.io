Christina Wan's represents the return of the Wan family to Fort Lauderdale. The Wan family previously offered it fare to guests in Fort Lauderdale at 1201 North Federal Highway from 1986 to 1993. Wan's has been a long standing South Florida tradition and favorite since 1966. Originally located on S.W. 8th street in Miami.. The family has continuously served guests at various locations in Miami, North Miami Beach, Hollywood, and Fort Lauderdale since its creation.
