Who Can Use 2-Way SMS?

School

Create a direct line of communication between teachers and the parents of their students by utilizing two-way texting. Schools can also combat bullying by setting up an account for students to text school officials -- 2-way SMS will allow officials to reply back directly to those students.

Church

Communicate with your congregation easier than ever before. Members of your church can opt-in to receive text messages and then you can communicate with them for special events such as fundraisers or charity drives -- ask for volunteers via text and they can use 2-way SMS to reply whether or not they are able to help out.

Business

Set up a direct link with your customers using SMS Broadcasts and two-way texting. Send out customized text messages to each customer asking for feedback and go through each reply from within your DialMyCalls account.
