Himmarshee Public House is downtown Fort Lauderdale’s newest concept from the Jey Hospitality Group.

This 5000 sq/ft state of the art restaurant and sports lounge is located in the center of Fort Lauderdale’s historic "Himmarshee Village." Boasting over 100 beers, hand crafted cocktails, 30 high definition flat screens, a giant LED wall and a private lounge for special events, HPH makes it the premier destination to have drinks or view any sporting event in town. The unique space also features the cities largest center bar with 40 draft beers and a "comfort zone" where you can relax on one of our large couches and enjoy the game or also host your next party or corporate event.

The cuisine at HPH specializes in global "Comfort Foods" with a modern twist. The menu is not only innovative but very diverse featuring an assortment of small plates meant for sharing, gourmet artisan flatbreads, street tacos and large plates. There is truly something for everyone.

Whether you stop by for a casual lunch, dinner, after work drink or want a big night out on the town HPH is the place to you.
