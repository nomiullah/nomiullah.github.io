South Florida's first sporting lounge

Featured in Esquire

September 28 2013

This 5000 sq/ft state of the art restaurant and sports lounge is located in the center of Fort Lauderdale’s historic “Himmarshee Village.” Boasting over 100 beers, hand crafted cocktails, 30 high definition flat screens, a giant LED wall and a private lounge for special events, HPH makes it the premier destination to have drinks or view any sporting event in town. The unique space also features the cities largest center bar with 40 draft everyone.
