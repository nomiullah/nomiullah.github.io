how is my digital fingerprint being tracked?

Your Digital Fingerprint

Online trackers scrape your web browser using cookies, or small files that store your location or browsing history.

These same trackers use the data from your cookies to create a digital "profile" that houses all of your online activity, including your shopping habits, your search history, and who you're connecting with online.

Armed with your digital fingerprint, advertisers, third parties and other ecommerce websites will start serving you ads based on your online activity. In the hands of nefarious hackers, your personal information is vulnerable to their use.

After building your profile, or "digital fingerprint," these trackers will sell it to the highest bidder to turn a profit.
