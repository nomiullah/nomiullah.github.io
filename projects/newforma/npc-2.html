Key features

Email management

File email with drag-and-drop ease from standard email clients. Add-in for Microsoft Office Outlook makes it easy to relate emails to action items, RFIs, and submittals without leaving the Outlook inbox.

comprehensive search

Indexes the project information on your network to return fast, comprehensive results via the Search function. Search across all or selected projects, including projects that pre-date your Newforma implementation.

web-enabled coordination

Provides an easy-to-use, secure means of distributing and receiving information, especially when in large file sizes. Provides secure web access to information, including record document revisions, synchronized project folders, action items and more.

EDMS Integration

Raise the return on your investment in electronic document management systems. Newforma allows you to check files in and out of Bentley ProjectWise, IBM FileNet, and Microsoft SharePoint directly from Newforma Project Center.

Newforma to Newforma

Automatically coordinate folders, files and models. Exchange RFIs, submittals and file transfers. Always work with the latest BIM and CAD files, reducing risks. Imposes none of the added costs and work of vaults and extranets. Respect each firmâs security settings.

Document control

Standardizes revision management of record documents issued for review, permitting and construction. Browse and interactively preview the most recent drawing revisions and graphically compare them to previously issued revisions.

Design Review

Share markups with everyone on the internal or external team. Capture and record markup sessions for a history of the design review process. Electronically compare document versions to identify all required or unintended changes.

Meeting minutes

Create, distribute and track agendas and minutes for project meetings. Action items, RFIs and other open items can be added as discussion topics to agendas and tracked across a series of meetings until resolved.

Project images

Interactively search, filter and group images by Date Taken, Camera Model, Location and other EXIF properties embedded by digital cameras. Images can be quickly tagged with Newforma keywords for future retrieval using Newforma Search.
