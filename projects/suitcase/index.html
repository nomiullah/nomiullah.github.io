Social Suitcase is a content management system for the modern digital age. With Social Suitcase powering your website you can quickly and easily author content and seamlessly integrate conversation from variety of social channels - turning your website into an entertaining, socially rich experience.

What's in Social Suitcase?

Social Suitcase has a number of powerful features that are manageable through an easy-to-use interface.

Enhance Your Content

Social Suitcase delivers an optimal content editing environment that allows you to effortlessly blend content with your website. By putting content first, Social Suitcase delivers features that turns websites into engaging content rich experiences, manageable from any device.

Own User Generated Content

Turn your audiences into fans and voices for your brand. Harness conversations by creating custom social media feeds from Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Vine etc. You can select what hashtags you would like your feed to focus on. You even have to ability to approve content before it appears in your feed.

Make Your Content More Social

Embed social media posts into your websites content. Through the drag-and-drop interface, you can easily add tweets, posts, Instagram photos and much more, to turn your content into engaging social stories.

Create Social Campaigns

-From competitions to polls, you have the ability to effortlessly create social campaigns that generate conversation, engagement and value with your audiences. Take hold of user generated content and use drive interest in your campaign through the power of social.
