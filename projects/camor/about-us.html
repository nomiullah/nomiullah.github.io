Camor Products Inc. was established in 1986 as an Intricate Industrial Pad Printer, plastic Injection Molder, and Small Sub Assembly provider. Over the years we have grown to include Hot Stamping, Ultra Sonic Welding/Inserting, Thermal inserting, Corona Surface Treating, and Electrical Assembly to CSA, UL, and other such agencies requirements after approval, Shrink Wrapping and Drop Shipping.

Our long term experienced staff is the assurance that your product will be processed to your exact specifications.

At Camor we believe our success is attributed to producing the highest quality molded, decorated parts and assemblies to a broad range of industries such as Consumer Electronics, Flag and Banner Industries, Automotive, Appliance, Medical, as well as Display and Promotion.

We do this consistently on time and within budget. By providing production from a full range of state of the art machines, we are capable of decorating parts of almost any shape or size. In fact, we have the capacity to decorate, assemble, package and ship your part… all from our facility. This means less damage, less freight, less worries; and more control over your project thereby saving you the customer time and money.

Our Promise to You

Uncompromised Quality – Our employees are continuously trained to respond to strict product specifications and embrace quality fundamentals throughout our entire production process.

Easily Adaptable to Small Runs – Camor maintains production lines that are designed specifically for fast product change overs and low production runs which allows for “just in time” production or detailing of parts.

Deep Understanding of Resins – Our experience with resins means that your products will benefit from our experience in molding, decorating and assembly of parts produced in engineered resins.

On Time Delivery – Delays cost money period. Camor understands your need for on time delivery and makes our clients’ needs a priority on every job.

Consistency – Onsite mold experts are ready to repair or make improvements to your tooling as needed.
