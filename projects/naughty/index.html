Methodology

Methodology steps

  • List of the top 100 online retailers was obtained from amazon Alexa’s top websites feature*.
  • A modified version of the publically available TrackOFF software was then used to scrape all 100 websites and collect all tracking scripts associated with each individual site.
  • The collected tracking scripts were stored in a local database and then exported to a CSV file for analysis by the TrackOFF team.
  • The team then ranked the sites by total tracking scripts present on each site.

* Note: The sites in the top sites lists are ordered by their 1 month Alexa traffic rank. The 1 month rank is calculated using a combination of average daily visitors and pageviews over the past month. The site with the highest combination of visitors and pageviews is ranked #1.
