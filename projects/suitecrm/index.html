HEBNA now offers pipeline operators the ability to effectively inspect steel pipe that has been lined with HDPE, or cement, or other internal coatings or liners in pipe sizes from 8” to 58“ diameter.

Advancements In Pipeline Corrosion Control

For years compression-fit HDPE polymer liners have been a cost-effective tool to control internal corrosion in ferrous pipelines. Recent advancements in materials, equipment and technologies have broadened polymer liner capabilities into areas previously inaccessible or impractical for polymer liner use.

This webinar will provide you with a better understanding of recent advancements and provide you with cost-effective solutions to better control corrosion in your pipeline assets. Covered topics will include:

  • Features and Benifits of HEBNA's new Extra Responsive / High Resolution Magnetic Flux Leakage Tool
  • New applications made possible by these advancements such as PHMSA regulated pipelines
  • Different types of polymer liner materials, material advancements and the broadened technical envelope
  • Condition assessment of previously installed polymer lining systems
  • Condition assessment of the host pipe behind the polymer liner
  • Case studies
