Corporate Secretarial Services

Under Section 171 of Singapore Companies Act Chapter 50, a company is required to appoint a company secretary within 6 months of its incorporation. And the office of Company Secretary cannot be left vacant for more than 6 months- Under Section 171 (4) , of Singapore Companies Act.

Our Service covers the following

  • Registration of a Company Secretary as required by the law
  • Filing of all required documents and forms with Accounting & Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA)
  • To maintain and update of the Company’s statutory registers and records
  • Ad-hoc assignments such as allotment and transfer of shares, amendments of M&A
  • All matters pertaining to Annual General Meeting
  • Assist to lodge company or officer changes to authority
