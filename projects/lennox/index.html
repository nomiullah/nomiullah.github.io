lennox welding since 1940

Lennox Welding and Supply was founded as a result of the Alberta oil boom in the 1940’s. Len Lennox and Ted Hunter could see the need for a local manufacturer of custom-made oilfield truck equipment.

Ted Hunter carried on the business after Len left the company in the early years. Ted’s sons Gord Hunter and Jim Hunter joined the business in the 1970’s. Their cousin Kent Hunter has worked alongside them since the 1980’s. Karen Hunter joined as office manager in the 1990's. Jim retired in 2000. Ken Moffitt joined the management team in 2003.
