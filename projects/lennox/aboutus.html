Lennox Welding and Supply has been operating in Edmonton, Alberta since 1953. We provide personalized service for all your oilfield truck manufacturing needs.

We build custom rigging for bed trucks, winch tractors, picker trucks, highway tractors and off-road equipment. Anything you can dream up, we can make real. Lennox Welding and Supply rigs up over 80 trucks each year. Rugged and ready to go to work.

Vehicles adapted for tough, varied climate conditions; from deep-freeze cold to blistering heat. You’ll find our work across Canada and the U.S., from Northern Alberta to Texas. Rigs with our stamp on them can be found working in oilfields around the globe: Australia, the Middle East, South East Asia, Russia, Africa and South America.

Installing winches, fifthwheels and other items, we represent the very best manufacturers, like Holland Hitch, ASA Electronics, the Braden and Tulsa Winch companies. We also supply hydraulics, air and electrical controls, driveline components and power take offs.

Our services include welding, winch repairs, mechanical winch drive assembly, complete hydraulic and electrical system design and custom-made control panels. We also offer hydraulic trouble-shooting. Let us get your rig on the road!

Lennox Welding and Supply was founded as a result of the Alberta oil boom in the 1940’s. Len Lennox and Ted Hunter could see the need for a local manufacturer of custom-made oilfield truck equipment.

Ted Hunter carried on the business after Len left the company in the early years. Ted’s sons Gord Hunter and Jim Hunter joined the business in the 1970’s. Their cousin Kent Hunter has worked alongside them since the 1980’s. Karen Hunter joined as office manager in the 1990's. Jim retired in 2000. Ken Moffitt joined the management team in 2003. Kent and Ken will carry on when Gord and Karen retire to do some serious golfing.

Starting with a one-bay shop in 1953, we’ve continually expanded to meet our customer’s needs. We now have more than 10,000 feet of shop and warehouse space. A second property was purchased to allow for more yard space. Lennox Welding and Supply now rigs out over 80 trucks a year.

Continued growth in our Parts Warehouse meets customer demand quickly. Holland Fifth Wheels and parts, Tulsa and Braden Winches, hydraulic valves and pumps, air controls and spare parts for everything we build are readily available.

Lennox Welding and Supply has established a solid reputation for innovation in specialized oilfield truck manufacturing. Simply put, the equipment works! Decade after decade. We’ve stood the test of time and our equipment will, too.
