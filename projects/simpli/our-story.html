    Homemade meals enrich lives through nutrition, money savings and the ritual of eating together.

Meaningful Meals

Sympli’s authentic African ingredients inspire home cooking and time together. Home-cooked food is more nutritious. It has less sugar, salt, unhealthy fat, fewer additives and preservatives.
Plus it costs less.

Sustainability

Post-harvest waste causes up to a third of farming production loss, a global issue. Symply Individually Quick-Frozen Technology means that more of a harvest can be saved by reducing spoilage and getting products to market more quickly. By reducing post-harvest waste, Symply helps African farmers become more efficient, increasing their income.

Authenticity

Sympli ingredients are sourced, produced and packaged in Africa for authentic flavors.

Natural

We don’t add unnecessary salt, sugar, fat, colour, flavor, fragrance or preservatives. We clearly state anyextra ingredients included to preserve freshness.

Quality

From selecting seeds to growing and harvesting produce to manufacturing products, Sympli ensures the highest quality. All Sympli products are Halal and Kosher certified.
