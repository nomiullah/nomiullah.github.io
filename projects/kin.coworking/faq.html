Frequently asked q’s

What is KIN?

KIN is a membership-based working space located in Montreal, Quebec. We are geared towards creatives and creators, whether they be freelancers, entrepreneurs, small businesses or just someone with a really great idea. KIN is also an events space open to members and the public.

What is included in a KIN membership?

Membership is billed on a monthly basis. There are two tiers of KIN membership

Tier 1 — SIBLING

Take full advantage of your KIN membership with a dedicated desk. It comes with a storage cabinet underneath, but you can feel free to leave your stuff on top. Either way, it’ll be safe. We’re family here.

Membership price: $_ monthly.

Tier 2 — COUSIN

You have a KIN membership, but hang out on your own terms. That means you have a hot desk and can float around wherever, whenever you like.

Membership price: $ _ monthly.
