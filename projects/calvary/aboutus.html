About us

Meet, Know, and Follow Jesus.

WELCOME TO CALVARY BAPTIST CHURCH

We exist as a church to glorify God by making true disciples of all people; disciples who manifest their maturity by exalting God in worship, by edifying and equipping believers for service, and evangelizing non-believers.

At Calvary Baptist Church, we believe that the Bible is God’s Word and that it holds the answers to all of life’s real questions. Our ministries are geared to all age levels and directed at specific areas of need. We seek to discover, to teach, and to live by the truth given to us by God in the Bible.

Thank you for taking the time to browse through our website. It is designed to introduce you to the various ministries of Calvary Baptist Church.

Our aim is to serve; therefore, if you have any questions or if we can help you in any way, please do not hesitate to call us at the number listed below. I am confident that we can help and also be an encouragement to you.
